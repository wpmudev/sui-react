<Section title="Alignment">

	<p>There are two ways to align the Checkbox: `Inline` and `Stacked`. 
		By default, Checkboxes are aligned on the top as Stack elements, but you can add one next to another as inline elements.</p>
	<p> Align your inputs on a single column towards the left side of the screen. Why? There are two reasons:</p>
	<p> 1. A single, vertical line is easier to follow.</p>
	<p> 2. Studies have shown that users in left-to-right languages tend to scan pages starting from the left part of the screen</p>
	<p> You can still use a horizontal layout, but your buttons and labels should be arranged so that users can tell which choice corresponds to which label without trouble.</p>
